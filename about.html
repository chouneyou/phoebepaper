Hi It's Phoebe!

I am a self-taught illustrator who started to draw things when I could hold a pen on hand.
My artwork is super based on my friends and family. Most of them comes from my real life, and little bit dreams. The color palatte usually is inspired by modern arts. Mostly, it is from Matisse.
My favorite artist is Mark Rothko.
I usually make them as home deco, postcards and stickers.
